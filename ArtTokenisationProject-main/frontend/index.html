How Token Creation Works

Transparent, secure, and simple - see exactly how your artwork becomes a blockchain asset

1

Submit Artwork Details

You fill out the form with your artwork information, artist details, and upload the artwork image. All data is validated and securely stored.

2

IPFS Upload

Your artwork image is uploaded to IPFS (InterPlanetary File System), a decentralized storage network. This ensures your art is permanently accessible and tamper-proof.

3

Smart Contract Creation

A unique smart contract is generated on the blockchain containing all your artwork's metadata - title, artist, creation date, dimensions, and IPFS link.

4

Blockchain Minting

Your token is minted on the blockchain, creating an immutable record of ownership and authenticity. This process is irreversible and permanently verifiable.

5

Token Certificate Generated

You receive a digital certificate with your unique Token ID, blockchain address, QR code, and all artwork details. Your art is now a verified blockchain asset!
